Chief's welcome

On behalf of the Northbridge Police Department, I would like to take this opportunity to welcome you to the official website of the Northbridge Police Department. This website offers the public an opportunity to better acquaint themselves with the Northbridge Police Department.

The employees of the Northbridge Police Department hope this website enhances the public's knowledge of the police department and the many services that the police department provides. I hope you will take an opportunity to thoroughly explore our website and take advantage of the many online services, information and links it provides to better serve our community.

This website allows you to email police officers assigned to the various divisions of our police department, please feel free to contact these officers with your questions, problems or concerns.

I hope that you find our website useful and informative.

New Northbridge Police Department Graduates

On January 21, 2011, Student Officers Brian Collins and Jarrod Woeller of the Northbridge Police Department graduated from the 3rd Municipal Police Training Academy held at the MA State Police Academy in New Braintree. Officers Collins and Woeller attended the 21 week recruit training academy covering a variety of police topics including domestic violence, applied patrol procedures, criminal and constitutional law, firearms, motor vehicle law, report writing, CPR & first aid and physical fitness.
